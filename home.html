About the project

In this project an IoT device which is a plant monitoring device was monitored, the device collects the environmental conditions surrounding the plan;
moisture, temperature, and light. These are vital factors for a plant's growth and well-being.
It is by manipulating them that we can influence the livelihood of a plant how we desire it grows.
